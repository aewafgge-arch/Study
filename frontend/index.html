Simple process

Four steps to swap

No complicated process. Register, list what you have, find what you need, and exchange.

01
👤
Create Account
Register with your college email and verify your student identity to join the campus community.
Click to Register →
02
📦
List Your Items
Upload books, notes, or devices. Choose to sell, donate, or swap. Set your price and category.
Click to List →
03
🔍
Search & Filter
Browse listings by subject, semester, or category. Filter by price, condition, and transaction type.
Click to Search →
04
🤝
Connect & Exchange
Message the seller directly. Arrange a safe on-campus meetup and complete the exchange.
Click to Connect →

Platform features

Everything you need to share smarter

Secure Student Login

Only verified students can access the platform. OTP-based authentication ensures every user is a real peer — creating a trusted community for all exchanges.
